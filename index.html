Discover

New Codes

Welcome to the new era of self-expression. Of convergence in fashion & media; blended identities & evolving social norms. It’s time to embrace play & immersion & augmentation. To explore new dimensions & transitions & unleash the power of community. The opportunity is right now. We are building New Codes

  • Shape the future
    of digital
    fashion


    Join us for this landmark event at the Royal Institution in London’s exclusive Mayfair district. Business leaders, creators, entrepreneurs, technologists, investors and advisers will gather to share valuable insights, build new networks and propel the industry in its next stage of evolution.

  • Join the
    conversation


    New Codes will engage with digital fashion at its most compelling. Whether your interests lie in generative AI, 3d design & animation, web3, digital luxury, gamification, avatars, next gen supply chains, sustainability, enterprise workflows, digital economies, new clienteling, phygital fashion, or digitally influenced physical fashion, there will be content and conversations for you, along with much more. Now’s the time to be “in the room”.

  • Explore digital
    identity


    New Codes will explore the social significance of digital fashion, unpacking the values of the generations that engage with it. How do trends & influence move from digital world to the physical and vice versa? What does effective brand-building & storytelling look like in the age of blended identity? What determines the value a digital or semi-digital garment?

  • Activations


    Watch this space for news of brand activations and other surprises. Expect to be inspired & entertained.
