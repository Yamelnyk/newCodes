About
the venue

New Codes will take place at the Royal Institution in the heart of London’s exclusive Mayfair district. Located amidst Mayfair’s luxury boutiques, tailors and high-end restaurants, the Institution is steeped in history and renowned for its Christmas science lectures. It was here the discovery of the electron and the existence of photography were announced amongst many other advances. Our speakers will join an intellectual tradition dating back to the early 1800s when they take the floor of the Institution's iconic lecture theater. We will take inspiration from this distinguished venue’s history as we look to the future of the fashion industry.

Getting There

The Royal Institution is a five minute walk from Green Park station on the Jubilee, Victoria and Piccadilly Lines. It is a ten minute walk from Piccadilly Circus or Oxford Circus stations. Bus numbers 9, 14, 19, 22 and 38 run along Piccadilly by the entrance to Albemarle Street.
